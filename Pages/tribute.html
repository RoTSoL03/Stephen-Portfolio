ANDY HARDING

"The Greatest Technician That's Ever Lived"

A personal appreciation tribute:


Andy Harding is a force of nature—relentless, passionate, and truly inspiring. In an industry that seems to be teetering on the edge of a new age, one driven by corporate greed and rapid change, Andy continues to push forward with unwavering dedication. Despite the challenges and the hurdles, he remains committed to informing his audience, sharing his deep knowledge of technology—especially in the world of PCs and laptops—through social media platforms that allow him to connect with and inspire so many.

His office, humble as it may be, is a testament to his resilience. It’s a small room tucked away behind the bustle of a subway station, but within that small space lies boundless creativity, an unwavering work ethic, and a heart that cares deeply about the people he reaches. He has my deepest respect—not just for his contributions to the tech world, but for his genuine kindness, humor, and passion that shine through in everything he does.

Known far and wide on the internet as "The Greatest Technician That’s Ever Lived," Andy’s reputation speaks for itself. Even when he "borrows" my toothbrush—though, it’s all part of his joke, since he cleverly repurposes old, used toothbrushes as cleaning brushes for his customers’ dusty computers—I can’t help but laugh and admire his resourcefulness. It’s just another example of his quirky sense of humor and his commitment to going the extra mile, even in the smallest details. His posts always bring a smile, and his wit makes every interaction with him feel personal and fun.

Andy Harding isn’t just a tech expert; he’s a source of joy, a reminder of the value of hard work, humor, and creativity. And for all of that, I’m beyond grateful.
